# Getting Started

Welcome to ExperienceQR! This guide will help you set up your account and create your first interactive digital experience.

## What is ExperienceQR?

ExperienceQR transforms any venue into an interactive guide through QR codes. Visitors scan a code and instantly access:

- Rich content (text, images, links)
- AI-powered voice conversations
- Multi-language support
- No app download required

**Perfect for:** Museums, restaurants, hotels, events, trade shows, tourist attractions, and any venue wanting to engage visitors digitally.

## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Sign Up

1. Visit the ExperienceQR website
2. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"**
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Verify your email address

You can also sign up using Google for faster access.

### Step 2: Choose Your Plan

After signing up, you'll start on the **Free plan**:

| Free Plan Includes |
|--------------------|
| Up to 3 projects |
| 50 visitor sessions per month |
| AI assistant feature |
| Basic analytics |

Upgrade anytime to unlock more projects, sessions, and translations.

## Creating Your First Project

### Step 1: Click "New Project"

From My Projects, click the **+ New Project** button.

### Step 2: Enter Basic Information

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Project Name** | What visitors will see | "City Museum Guide" |
| **Description** | Brief overview | "Explore our exhibits with AI assistance" |
| **Cover Image** | Visual for your project | Upload a photo |

### Step 3: Choose Display Mode

Select how your content will appear to visitors:

| Mode | When to Use |
|------|-------------|
| **Single** | One main piece of content (article, announcement) |
| **List** | Multiple items in a scrollable list |
| **Grid** | Visual gallery with 2-column layout |
| **Cards** | Featured items with large images |

### Step 4: Add Content

Add your content items:

1. Click **"Add Content Item"**
2. Enter a title and description
3. Add an image (optional)
4. Add a link (optional)
5. Repeat for each item

### Step 5: Generate QR Code

1. Go to the **"QR Codes"** tab
2. Click **"Generate QR Code"**
3. Download or print your QR code
4. Place it where visitors can scan

## Testing Your Experience

Before sharing with visitors:

1. **Scan the QR code** with your phone
2. **Browse the content** as a visitor would
3. **Test the AI assistant** if enabled
4. **Check different languages** if translations are set up

## Tips for Success

### Content Best Practices

- **Keep titles short** - Visitors scan quickly
- **Use high-quality images** - They make a big difference
- **Write concise descriptions** - Mobile screens are small
- **Organize logically** - Group related items together

### QR Code Placement

- **Eye level** - Where visitors naturally look
- **Good lighting** - So cameras can scan easily
- **Clear instructions** - "Scan for digital guide"
- **Multiple locations** - Entrance, key exhibits, exit

### AI Assistant Tips

- **Be specific** - Clear instructions produce better responses
- **Add knowledge** - The more context, the smarter the AI
- **Test thoroughly** - Try questions visitors might ask

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do visitors need to download an app?

No. Visitors simply scan the QR code and the experience opens in their web browser.

### How many visitors can access my project?

Depends on your plan:
- **Free**: 50 sessions/month
- **Starter**: ~800-1,600 sessions/month
- **Premium**: ~7,000-14,000 sessions/month

### Can I update content after creating the QR code?

Yes! QR codes link to your project, not the content itself. Update content anytime and visitors see the changes immediately.

### What languages are supported?

The AI assistant and translations support 10 languages:
- English, Traditional Chinese, Simplified Chinese
- Japanese, Korean, Spanish, French
- Russian, Arabic, Thai
