# Traditional Chinese Natural Language Update - November 17, 2025

## Overview
Revised Traditional Chinese translations to feel more natural and comfortable for native Chinese speakers, moving away from literal translations to idiomatic expressions and colloquial phrasing.

## Philosophy

### Translation Approach Changed
**Before**: Direct translation from English (literal, formal)
**After**: Native-feeling Chinese (natural, conversational)

### Key Principles
1. **口語化 (Colloquial)**: Use everyday language Chinese speakers actually use
2. **簡潔有力 (Concise & Punchy)**: Short, impactful phrases
3. **親切感 (Friendly tone)**: Make readers feel comfortable
4. **在地化 (Localized)**: Cultural fit for Taiwan/Hong Kong/Macau readers

## Major Changes by Section

### 1. Hero Section (主標題)

**Before (Literal translation):**
- "以 AI 驅動數位紀念卡轉變訪客體驗"
- "實體收藏卡結合 QR 碼啟動的 AI 語音互動"

**After (Natural Chinese):**
- "AI 智能紀念卡打造全新觀展體驗"
- "精美收藏卡搭配 AI 語音導覽"
- "掃碼即用，免下載 App"

**Why it's better:**
- "智能" is more familiar than "驅動" in Chinese
- "打造" feels more active and engaging
- "搭配" is natural spoken Chinese
- "掃碼即用" is a popular internet phrase everyone knows
- "免下載 App" sounds more casual and friendly

### 2. Demo Section (演示)

**Before:**
- "查看運作方式"
- "互動演示卡配有實時 QR 碼"
- "使用任何智慧型手機掃描"

**After:**
- "看看怎麼用" (How everyday people talk)
- "掃描 QR 碼，手機立即體驗"
- "任何手機都能掃，免下載 App"

**Natural phrases added:**
- "馬上試試" (Try it right now - very colloquial)
- "手機掃碼" (Everyone says this, not "使用手機掃描")
- "隨時回味" (Savor the memories - poetic but natural)

**Why it's better:**
- "看看怎麼用" = how people actually ask "how does this work?"
- "都能掃" is more inclusive and friendly than "可以掃描"
- Natural rhythm and flow in Chinese

### 3. How It Works (使用方式)

**Before:**
- "簡單三步驟流程"
- "從購買到持久記憶——專為無縫訪客互動而設計"

**After:**
- "使用超簡單"
- "三個步驟，從購買到永久珍藏"

**Step descriptions transformed:**
1. "訪客在現場購買精美紀念卡" → Simple, direct
2. "手機掃描 QR 碼，立即進入數位世界" → Evocative "digital world"
3. "實體卡片隨身帶，數位內容隨時看" → Rhyming cadence (隨身帶/隨時看)

**Why it's better:**
- "超簡單" = super simple (internet-speak, very popular)
- Rhyming makes it memorable
- "進入數位世界" is more exciting than "存取內容"

### 4. Features Section (功能)

**Before (Formal):**
- "實體 + 數位卡片"
- "連結豐富數位內容和 AI 導覽的 QR 碼收藏品"
- "適用於任何智慧型手機。最大無障礙性，零摩擦。"

**After (Natural):**
- "實體卡 + 數位內容" (Simpler word order)
- "精美收藏卡連結豐富數位內容和 AI 導覽" (Added "精美" for appeal)
- "任何手機都能用，零門檻" (More colloquial)

**Standout changes:**
- "免下載 App" vs "無需應用程式" (Way more natural!)
- "一站搞定" = One-stop solution (popular internet phrase)
- "零門檻" = Zero barrier (common Chinese expression)
- "場館零成本" = Venue pays nothing (Clear benefit statement)

**Why it's better:**
- "搞定" is everyday Chinese for "get it done"
- "零門檻" resonates more than "零摩擦"
- Feels like a friend explaining, not a manual

### 5. Pricing Section (定價)

**Before:**
- "簡單透明定價"
- "無隱藏費用。無訪客計費。僅支付實體卡片費用。"

**After:**
- "定價超清楚"
- "只收卡片費用，不收訪客費用，沒有隱藏費用。"

**Why it's better:**
- "超清楚" = super clear (more casual, friendly)
- "只收...不收..." = Only charge... don't charge... (Natural Chinese rhythm)
- Shorter, punchier sentences

### 6. FAQ Section (常見問題)

This section got the biggest transformation!

**Q1: "什麼是 CardStudio？" → "CardStudio 是什麼？"**
- More natural question order in Chinese

**Answers transformed to conversational style:**

**Before:**
- "CardStudio 是一個結合實體收藏卡和 AI 驅動體驗的數位紀念品平台"

**After:**
- "把實體紀念卡和數位體驗結合在一起的平台"

**Q2 - Changed to numbered list with casual language:**
- "1) 訪客在現場買卡"
- "2) 手機掃 QR 碼"
- "3) 馬上開始用 AI 語音導覽"
- "4) 卡片留著收藏，內容隨時可以看"

**Q3: Natural yes/no question:**
- "不用。任何手機的瀏覽器都能直接打開，掃碼就能用。"

**Q5: Very casual explanation:**
- "全包：AI 語音系統、後台管理、多語言翻譯..."
- "您只付卡片製作費，每張 2 美元。"

**Q7: Super casual:**
- "當然可以。用我們的後台就能自己設計..."
- "隨時都能改。"

**Q8: Colloquial timing:**
- "印刷約 4 個工作天，寄送看地點約 5-10 天。"
- "看地點" = depends on location (very casual)

**Q9: Natural explanation:**
- "最少 {min} 張起訂，這樣製作成本才划算。"
- "才划算" = then it's cost-effective (everyday expression)

**Last line:**
- "還有問題？填寫聯絡表單跟我們聊聊。"
- "聊聊" = chat (super friendly, not "聯繫")

**Why it's better:**
- Reads like a real person answering questions
- Uses common expressions like "馬上", "當然", "聊聊"
- Natural cadence and rhythm
- Feels approachable, not corporate

### 7. Contact Section (聯絡)

**Before:**
- "申請試點計畫、探索合作夥伴關係或提出問題。我們將在 24-48 小時內回覆。"

**After:**
- "想試用、談合作、有疑問？我們會在 24-48 小時內回覆您。"

**Why it's better:**
- "想...？" = Wanna...? (More inviting)
- Three short parallel phrases (想試用、談合作、有疑問)
- "會...回覆您" is more personal than "將...回覆"

### 8. Footer (頁腳)

**Before:**
- "為全球場館和景點提供 AI 驅動體驗的數位紀念品平台。"

**After:**
- "結合實體紀念卡與 AI 智能導覽，服務全球博物館和景點的數位平台。"

**Why it's better:**
- Starts with "結合" (combine) - more active
- "智能導覽" sounds better than "驅動體驗"
- More specific: mentions museums and attractions

## Key Vocabulary Choices

### Everyday Expressions Used
| Formal/Literal | Natural Chinese | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 使用智慧型手機掃描 | 手機掃碼 | Scan with phone |
| 無需應用程式 | 免下載 App | No app needed |
| 即時、無需應用程式存取 | 掃碼即用 | Instant QR access |
| 試用即時演示 | 馬上試試 | Try live demo |
| 立即存取 | 馬上開始用 | Access instantly |
| 理解上下文並回答後續問題 | 理解上下文，回答追問 | Understands context |
| 適用於任何智慧型手機 | 任何手機都能用 | Works on any phone |
| 最大無障礙性，零摩擦 | 零門檻 | Zero barriers |
| 用於內容管理、分析和卡片發行的自助式平台 | 內容管理、數據分析、卡片發行一站搞定 | All-in-one platform |
| 聯繫我們討論 | 跟我們聊聊 | Contact us |

### Internet-Style Phrases (親民化)
- **超簡單** (super simple)
- **超清楚** (super clear)  
- **一站搞定** (one-stop solution)
- **零門檻** (zero barriers)
- **馬上試試** (try it now)
- **隨時都能改** (change anytime)

### Casual Connecting Words
- **當然可以** (of course) - friendly affirmation
- **不用** (no need) - casual negative
- **約** (about/around) - casual approximation
- **看地點** (depends on location) - very colloquial
- **才划算** (then it's worthwhile) - natural reasoning

## Testing Recommendations

### Read-Aloud Test
Have native speakers read it aloud. If it sounds like natural speech, it's good!

### Comprehension Test
- Can readers understand without re-reading?
- Does it feel comfortable and familiar?
- Would they use these phrases themselves?

### Emotional Test
- Does it feel welcoming?
- Would they want to try the product?
- Does it build trust?
