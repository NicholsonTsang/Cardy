## Translation Notes

### Terminology Choices
- **數位紀念品平台** (Digital souvenir platform) - chosen over "互動式紀念卡" for platform concept
- **訪客** (visitor) - consistent use throughout
- **場館** (venue) - more general than specific types
- **AI 語音助理** (AI voice assistant) - natural Traditional Chinese phrasing
- **無障礙** (accessibility) - standard term for inclusive access
- **自助式平台** (self-service platform) - clear for admin dashboard concept
