## Subscription Plans

ExperienceQR offers flexible subscription plans to fit your needs.

## Plan Comparison

### Free Tier

Perfect for getting started and small venues.

**Included:**
- Up to 3 projects
- 50 monthly access (visitor scans)
- Basic AI assistant
- Single language content
- QR code generation

**Limitations:**
- No multi-language translations
- No premium support
- Limited analytics

### Premium Tier - $50/month

Best for growing venues and professional use.

**Included:**
- Up to 15 projects
- 3,000 monthly access pool
- Advanced AI assistant with voice
- **Free unlimited translations** (10 languages)
- Priority support
- Full analytics dashboard
- Batch overage purchasing

## Monthly Access Pool

The access pool represents the number of times visitors can scan QR codes and access your content each month.

| Plan | Monthly Access |
|------|---------------|
| Free | 50 |
| Premium | 3,000 |

:::info Pooled Access
Access is pooled across all your projects. If you have 3 projects, they share the same monthly access allowance.
:::

## Daily Access Limits

In addition to the monthly pool, you can set **daily access limits** per project to:
- Prevent unexpected usage spikes
- Protect against abuse
- Distribute access evenly throughout the month

Configure daily limits in each project's settings.

## Overage Options (Premium Only)

If you exceed your monthly access limit:

- **Batch Purchase**: 5 credits = 100 additional access
- Credits never expire
- Purchase as needed

## Upgrading Your Plan

To upgrade to Premium:

1. Navigate to Plan & Usage in your dashboard
2. Click "Upgrade to Premium"
3. Complete payment via Stripe
4. Access premium features immediately

![Upgrade Button](/Image/docs/upgrade-plan.png "Upgrade to Premium")

## Billing Cycle

- **Monthly billing**: Charged on the same date each month
- **Proration**: If upgrading mid-cycle, pay the prorated difference
- **Cancellation**: Access continues until end of billing period

## Cancel Anytime

Premium subscriptions can be cancelled anytime:

1. Go to Plan & Usage
2. Click "Manage Subscription"
3. Select "Cancel Subscription"
4. Confirm cancellation

:::warning Downgrade Effects
When cancelling Premium:
- Translations become read-only (can view but not add new)
- Projects over the free limit become inaccessible
- Monthly access resets to free tier limit
:::
