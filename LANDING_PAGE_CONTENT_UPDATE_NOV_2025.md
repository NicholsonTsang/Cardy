## Content Writing Principles Applied

### 1. Conciseness
- Hero subtitle: 36 words → 20 words (44% reduction)
- Demo subtitle: 19 words → 13 words (32% reduction)
- Feature descriptions: Average 20 words → 10 words (50% reduction)
- FAQ answers: Average 40 words → 25 words (38% reduction)

### 2. Clarity
- Removed marketing jargon ("Ignite", "Living Stories", "Future-Proof")
- Replaced vague terms with specific benefits
- Used parallel structure for consistency
- Added numbered steps for complex processes

### 3. Professional Tone
- Changed from promotional to solution-focused
- Removed hyperbole and emotional language
- Used direct, factual statements
- Maintained benefits without overselling

### 4. Consistency
- Standardized all feature descriptions to 8-12 words
- Consistent structure across sections
- Parallel phrasing (verb + benefit)
- Uniform voice throughout

### 5. Action-Oriented
- Changed CTAs to be more specific ("See Demo" vs "Learn More")
- Added concrete timelines ("24-48 hours", "2-3 weeks")
- Focused on what users can do now
- Removed passive voice
